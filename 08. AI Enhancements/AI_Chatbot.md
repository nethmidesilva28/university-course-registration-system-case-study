# AI Student Support Chatbot

## Overview

An AI-powered chatbot can provide instant support to students during the registration process.

## Supported Functions

- Course registration FAQs
- Registration deadlines
- Course prerequisite guidance
- Timetable inquiries
- Registration status assistance

## Benefits

- 24/7 student support
- Reduced administrative workload
- Faster response times
- Improved user experience

## Example Questions

- "How do I register for a course?"
- "What courses are available this semester?"
- "Why was my registration rejected?"
