# Project Objectives

## Primary Objective

To design a centralized University Course Registration System which simplifies and automates the course enrollment process for students, academic advisors, lecturers, and administrators.

## Business Objectives

* Reduce course registration processing time.
* Minimize manual administrative work.
* Improve accuracy of registration records.
* Enhance the overall student registration experience.
* Provide real-time access to course availability and registration status.
* Support efficient management of academic offerings.

## Project Objectives

* Develop an online course registration platform.
* Enable students to register and drop courses through a portal.
* Implement prerequisite and timetable conflict validation.
* Provide advisor approval workflows where required.
* Generate enrollment and registration reports for administrators.
* Ensure secure access through user authentication and role-based permissions.

## Success Criteria

* Registration processing time reduced by at least 80%.
* Significant reduction in registration-related errors.
* Increased student satisfaction with the registration process.
* Successful handling of peak registration periods without performance degradation.
* Improved visibility and reporting capabilities for academic administrators.
