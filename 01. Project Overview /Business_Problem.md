# Business Problem

The university currently manages course registration through a combination of paper forms, emails, and manual approval processes. This approach creates several operational and timing challenges for students, academic staff, and administrators.

Students often experience delays in registration approval, difficulty identifying available courses, timetable conflicts, and limited visibility into their registration status. Administrative staff spend significant time processing registrations manually, increasing the risk of data entry errors and inconsistencies.

As student enrollment continues to grow, the existing process becomes increasingly difficult to manage efficiently. The lack of automation and centralized data management negatively impacts both operational performance and the overall student experience.

To address these challenges, the university requires a centralized Course Registration System that automates registration workflows, improves data accuracy, provides real-time information, and supports efficient academic administration.
