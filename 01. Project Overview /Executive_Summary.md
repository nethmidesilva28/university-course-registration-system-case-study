# Executive Summary

The University Course Registration System is a digital solution designed to modernize the academic course enrollment process. The existing registration process relies heavily on manual procedures, resulting in delays, administrative inefficiencies, registration errors, and limited visibility for students.

This project proposes a centralized web-based platform that enables students to browse available courses, register for subjects, view schedules, and track registration statuses online rather than manual updates. Academic advisors and administrators will be able to review registrations, manage course offerings, and generate enrollment reports through their dedicated interfaces.

The proposed solution aims to improve operational efficiency, reduce manual workloads, enhance student experience, and ensure data accuracy throughout the registration lifecycle. Additionally, the project explores opportunities for Artificial Intelligence (AI) integration, including course recommendations, enrollment forecasting, and automated student support.

This case study demonstrates the application of Business Analysis, Requirements Engineering, Process Improvement, and Project Management principles within a higher education environment.
