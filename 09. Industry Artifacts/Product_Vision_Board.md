# Product Vision Board

## Vision

Create a seamless and efficient course registration experience for university students and staff.

## Target Users

- Students
- Academic Advisors
- Lecturers
- Registrar Office

## User Needs

- Fast registration process
- Real-time course information
- Easy approval workflows
- Accurate academic records

## Product Features

- Online Registration
- Timetable Generation
- Approval Management
- Reporting Dashboard

## Business Value

- Improved operational efficiency
- Better student experience
- Reduced administrative costs
- Increased data accuracy
