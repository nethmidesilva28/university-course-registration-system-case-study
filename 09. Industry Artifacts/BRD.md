# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

University Course Registration System

## Business Objective

To digitize and streamline the university course registration process, reducing administrative workload and improving the student registration experience.

## Business Problem

The current registration process relies on manual forms and approvals, resulting in delays, registration errors, and limited visibility for students and administrators.

## Business Requirements

### BR-01

Students must be able to register for courses online.

### BR-02

Academic advisors must be able to review and approve registrations.

### BR-03

Administrators must be able to manage course offerings.

### BR-04

The system must automatically validate prerequisites.

### BR-05

The system must generate registration reports.

## Success Metrics

* 80% reduction in registration processing time.
* Reduced registration errors.
* Improved student satisfaction.
* Increased administrative efficiency.
