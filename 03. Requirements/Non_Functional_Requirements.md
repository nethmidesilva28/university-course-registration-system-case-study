# Non-Functional Requirements

## Overview

Non-functional requirements define the quality attributes and operational standards of the system.

## Performance

### NFR-01

The system shall respond to user requests within 3 seconds under normal operating conditions.

### NFR-02

The system shall support up to 10,000 concurrent users during peak registration periods.

## Availability

### NFR-03

The system shall maintain an uptime of at least 99%.

## Security

### NFR-04

All users shall be authenticated using secure university credentials.

### NFR-05

Role-based access control shall restrict access according to user roles.

### NFR-06

Sensitive data shall be encrypted during transmission and storage.

## Usability

### NFR-07

The system shall provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface.

### NFR-08

The system shall be accessible from desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## Reliability

### NFR-09

The system shall automatically back up data daily.

### NFR-10

The system shall recover from failures with minimal data loss.
