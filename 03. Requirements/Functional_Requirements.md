# Functional Requirements

## Overview

Functional requirements define the features and capabilities that the University Course Registration System must provide to its users.

## Student Functions

### FR-01: User Authentication

Students shall be able to log in using university-issued credentials.

### FR-02: View Available Courses

Students shall be able to browse available courses for the selected semester.

### FR-03: Register for Courses

Students shall be able to enroll in available courses.

### FR-04: Drop Courses

Students shall be able to remove previously selected courses before the registration deadline.

### FR-05: View Timetable

Students shall be able to view their personalized class schedule.

### FR-06: Track Registration Status

Students shall be able to view pending, approved, or rejected registrations.

## Academic Advisor Functions

### FR-07: Review Registrations

Academic advisors shall be able to review student course selections.

### FR-08: Approve or Reject Registrations

Academic advisors shall be able to approve or reject registration requests.

## Lecturer Functions

### FR-09: View Enrolled Students

Lecturers shall be able to access class lists for their assigned courses.

## Administrator Functions

### FR-10: Manage Courses

Administrators shall be able to create, update, and deactivate courses.

### FR-11: Manage Academic Terms

Administrators shall be able to configure semesters and registration periods.

### FR-12: Generate Reports

Administrators shall be able to generate enrollment and registration reports.
