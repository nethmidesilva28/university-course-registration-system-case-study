# Stakeholder Map

## Purpose

The stakeholder map identifies individuals and groups affected by the University Course Registration System and evaluates their influence and interest in the project.

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder           | Role                                      | Interest Level | Influence Level |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------------- | --------------- |
| Students              | Primary system users                      | High           | Medium          |
| Academic Advisors     | Approve registrations                     | High           | High            |
| Lecturers             | Access enrolled student lists             | Medium         | Medium          |
| Registrar Office      | Manage registrations and academic records | High           | High            |
| University Management | Monitor performance and enrollment trends | Medium         | High            |
| IT Department         | Develop and maintain the system           | High           | High            |

## Power-Interest Grid

### Manage Closely

* Academic Advisors
* Registrar Office
* IT Department

### Keep Satisfied

* University Management

### Keep Informed

* Students
* Lecturers

### Monitor

* External University Stakeholders (if applicable)

## Key Observations

Students are the primary beneficiaries of the system, while the Registrar Office and Academic Advisors have the highest operational influence. The IT Department plays a critical role in successful implementation and maintenance of the solution.
